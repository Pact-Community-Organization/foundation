# Governance

The Pact Community Organization (PCO) follows a lightweight, open governance model to ensure transparency, inclusivity, and accountability.

## Roles and Responsibilities

### Director
- Strategic oversight and mission alignment.
- Approves governance changes and major decisions.
- Public representation of the organization.

### Maintainers
- Expert contributors who review and approve PRs.
- Ensure code quality and compliance with standards.
- Maintain the integrity of the Pact Contract Catalog.

### Contributors
- Anyone who submits issues, PRs, or improvements.
- Suggest changes, fix bugs, and improve documentation.

### Advisors
- Optional external experts (security, legal, technical).
- Provide guidance on specialized topics.

## Decision-Making Process

1. **Simple Changes**: Approved by maintainers.
2. **Contract Additions or Breaking Changes**: Majority maintainer agreement.
3. **Governance Changes**: Director approval.
4. **New Programs or Strategic Shifts**: Community proposal + Director approval.

## Community Proposals

Community members can submit proposals for new initiatives, programs, or changes to governance. Proposals should be submitted as GitHub issues in this repository.
