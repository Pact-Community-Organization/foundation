# Governance

The Pact Community Organization (PCO) follows a Hybrid governance model (Minimum Viable Governance â€” MVG) with room to extend as the community grows. This ensures transparency, inclusivity, and accountability while maintaining flexibility for scaling.

## Roles and Responsibilities

### Director
- Strategic oversight and mission alignment.
- Approves governance changes and major decisions.
- Public representation of the organization.

### Core Maintainers
- Hold merge rights for core repositories.
- Responsible for releases and major policy decisions.
- Ensure code quality and compliance with standards.
- Maintain the integrity of the Pact Contract Catalog.

### Council
- Small advisory body for governance decisions and conflicts.
- Initially appointed by the founding steward (`DaisukeFlowers`), later elected via RFC process.

### Security Working Group (WG)
- Triages security reports and coordinates responses.

### Contributors
- Anyone who submits issues, PRs, or improvements.
- Suggest changes, fix bugs, and improve documentation.
- Contributors may be promoted to maintainers via nomination.

### Advisors
- Optional external experts (security, legal, technical).
- Provide guidance on specialized topics.

## Decision-Making Process

1. **Simple Changes**: Approved by maintainers.
2. **Contract Additions or Breaking Changes**: Majority maintainer agreement.
3. **Governance Changes**: Director approval.
4. **New Programs or Strategic Shifts**: Community proposal + Director approval.
5. **High-Impact Changes**: Require an RFC and a Council review period (14 days) prior to merge.

## Community Proposals

Community members can submit proposals for new initiatives, programs, or changes to governance. Proposals should be submitted as GitHub issues in this repository.

## Appointments and Elections

- Initial maintainers and Council members are appointed by the founding steward (`DaisukeFlowers`).
- The RFC process will include guidance for elections and nominations when community size warrants.

## Conflict Resolution

- Conflicts should be reported to `info@pact-community.org`.
- The Council will mediate and provide resolutions.

## Amendments

This governance document may be amended via an RFC and majority approval by the Council.
