# Pact Community Organization Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the key milestones and priorities for the Pact Community Organization. It helps contributors, adopters, and community members understand our focus and progress.

---

## **Foundation Setup**
- Set up the foundation repository with Wiki and GitHub Pages.
- Publish **Mission, Vision, About, and Contribute** pages.
- Establish project board (Kanban style) with user stories and issues.
- Define roles and responsibilities for maintainers and contributors.

---

## **Community Infrastructure**
- Launch GitHub Discussions with categories: Questions, Ideas, Proposals.
- Add **issue and pull request templates**.
- Set up CODEOWNERS for Wiki and Pages.
- Promote channels for community engagement (Discord, social links).

---

## **Documentation & Standards**
- Draft initial **style guides, security standards, and naming conventions**.
- Publish “good first issue” and contributor-friendly tasks.
- Begin documenting initial **contract categories** (fungible tokens, NFTs, escrow, wallet, etc.).
- Ensure Wiki and Pages are synchronized and up to date.

---

## **Core Community Content**
- Expand Wiki with detailed guides and examples.
- Add curated **starter templates for developers**.
- Encourage community contributions through pull requests and discussions.
- Track adoption metrics (stars, forks, contributors).

---

## **Engagement & Growth**
- Host community calls or workshops to showcase progress.
- Promote “how to contribute” and highlight examples of adoption.
- Review contributed content for alignment with **mission and vision**.
- Identify high-priority issues for enterprise-ready modules.

---

## **Foundation Milestones**
- Complete first batch of **curated starter templates**.
- Publish adoption guide for businesses.
- Review and update Wiki/Pages content for clarity and completeness.
- Celebrate first contributors and community milestones.
- Plan objectives for future phases based on feedback and lessons learned.

---

## **Key Principles**
- **Mission-Driven:** Every task aligns with “making it easy and safe for businesses to start building with Pact.”
- **Minimal Viable Foundation:** Focus on documentation, community onboarding, and governance first.
- **Iterative Growth:** Build incrementally and encourage community participation from the start.
- **Transparency:** All progress, updates, and decisions are public and accessible.

---

*This roadmap is a living document and will be updated regularly to reflect progress, feedback, and evolving priorities.*
