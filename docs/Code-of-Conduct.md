# Pact Community Organization – Code of Conduct

The Pact Community Organization is committed to building a safe, welcoming, and collaborative environment for everyone.  
Our mission is to make it easy and safe for businesses to start building with Pact — and that begins with how we treat each other.

This Code of Conduct applies to all Foundation spaces, including:
- GitHub repositories and Wikis  
- Issues, pull requests, and discussions  
- Community forums, chats, and meetings  
- Any interaction under the Foundation’s name

By participating, you agree to uphold these standards.

---

## 1. Our Values

### **Respect**
Treat everyone with courtesy. Healthy debate is encouraged, but personal attacks or dismissive behavior is not.

### **Inclusivity**
We welcome individuals from all backgrounds, regardless of experience level, identity, or perspective.

### **Transparency**
We strive to communicate openly, share knowledge freely, and collaborate in good faith.

### **Safety**
We provide a community where people feel comfortable asking questions, contributing, and learning.

### **Mission Alignment**
Contributions should support the Foundation’s goal:
> *To make it easy and safe for businesses to start building with Pact.*

---

## 2. Expected Behavior

Participants must:
- Act respectfully and professionally.  
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.  
- Assume good intent when interpreting feedback or discussions.  
- Provide constructive feedback and accept constructive feedback gracefully.  
- Follow project maintainers’ decisions and guidance.  
- Stay focused on collaboration, not competition.

---

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors will not be tolerated:
- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind.  
- Discriminatory or insulting comments.  
- Personal or political attacks.  
- Threats or encouragement of violence.  
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or collaboration.  
- Posting malicious code, spam, or purposely misleading information.  
- Sharing private information without consent (doxing).

---

## 4. Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:
- Report it directly to the Foundation maintainers through GitHub (Issues or Discussions), or  
- Contact a maintainer privately if the issue is sensitive.

All reports will be handled discreetly and seriously.

---

## 5. Enforcement

Maintainers may take action that includes:
- Warning the offender  
- Removing comments or blocking disruptive behavior  
- Temporarily or permanently banning contributors  
- Escalating to GitHub Trust & Safety for severe violations  

Decisions made by Foundation maintainers are final.

---

## 6. A Living Document

The Code of Conduct may evolve as the Foundation grows.  
Community feedback is welcome — this document will be improved collaboratively.

---

By contributing to the Pact Community Organization, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct and help maintain a positive and productive community.
